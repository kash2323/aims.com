AIMS Faculty

Building on Miami University's commitment to excellent undergraduate education, AIMS faculty work with students to advise them in a tailored path through the curriculum. Faculty also run various international programs to work with clients in other cultures (past trips have included workshops in China, Ireland, and the United Kingdom). And faculty coach students during key industry opportunities, including the annual Game Developers Conference.
